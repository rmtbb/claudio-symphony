Welcome to Claudio 🎼

It turns your Claude Code session into calm, generative music. Every tool call, file edit, and finished reply becomes a soft, in-key sound — so you can hear what your agent is doing without staring at it.

🌌
Watch it play
Each glowing orb is an instrument — a voice — that blooms when Claude triggers it. Big moments (a finished turn) break across the screen as light. Click any orb to hear it; try the views ✦◍≈❋✸ top-right.
🎚️
Make it yours
The Sounds tab shows what plays when. Swap sounds, switch between 37 rooms, or build your own. Changes are live — nothing to save.
🎤
Jam with it
Hit Listen and it tunes to whatever you hum. Add a drone, a chord progression, headphones — and play along.
